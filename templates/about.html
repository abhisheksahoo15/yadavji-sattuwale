About Us

We are a family-run business from Bihar, passionately serving nutritious and tasty **Sattu** to the entire nation. With our roots in tradition and eyes on innovation, we bring you pocket-friendly health drinks with modern delivery methods. Our mission is to make every sip nostalgic, healthy, and satisfying.

Started by Yadav Ji in 1998, our recipe is pure, our chana is roasted to perfection, and our hearts are full of gratitude for your trust!
